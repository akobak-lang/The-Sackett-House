The Space

A sun-filled retreat in the heart
of Park Slope

Welcome to The Sackett House — a beautifully renovated 3-bedroom, 2-bathroom home set within a stunning brick townhouse on one of Park Slope's most charming residential blocks. The apartment occupies a private floor of the building, offering an uncommon sense of calm, light, and quality in the heart of Brooklyn.

Every room has been designed with care: statement bathrooms with custom tile work, warm wide-plank hardwood floors throughout, and sun flooding in from multiple exposures. The dual-zone HVAC keeps the whole home comfortable year-round — with the primary suite on its own independent climate control for a truly personalized stay.

Connectivity is first-class: Gigabit Fios fiber makes this an ideal base for remote workers, relocating professionals, or anyone who needs their home office to perform flawlessly. A dedicated desk in the primary suite adds to the work-from-home setup. Entry is effortless via keypad and smartphone — no keys to manage.

Please note: this is a 3rd floor walkup with no elevator. The home is perfectly suited for guests comfortable with stairs.

Park Slope is one of Brooklyn's most beloved neighborhoods — tree-lined streets, boutique restaurants, the Brooklyn Public Library, and Prospect Park just around the corner. The R train at 4th Avenue is a 5-minute walk, and Atlantic Terminal (A/C/G/2/3/4/5/B/D/N/Q/R) is about 15 minutes — putting all of Manhattan within easy reach.

A spacious king bedroom with a dedicated work desk — ideal for remote work or a longer stay. Light blue-grey walls, wall-mounted TV, wood-tone furniture, and bright street-facing window light. Twin Bedroom
